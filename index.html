Valley Titans AFC

Welcome to Valley Titans AFC home page. Formed in 1985 the club has gone from strength to strength over the years and looks forward to your continued support. Feel free to browse our website in order to get the latest news, buy tickets, watch highlights and goals plus many many more.

The News

New Signing

Valley Titans are pleased to announce the signing of German playmaker Mesut Ozil. Ozil joins the club on a 4-year contract for a fee of around £50 million from London side Arsenal and will be immediately available for selection.

During the press conference Ozil stated he was looking forward to meeting up with his new teammates for training and is relishing the new opportunity. Ozil stated his desire to see the club reach champions league and hopes he can help the club achieve this goal.

The boss had his say on the matter stating “Mesut is someone we have been watching for a while and the kind of player I admire.” The boss was quick to point out Ozil will be filling a hole left by the recent departure of club favourite Alexis Sanchez. He sated “Mesut’s creativity and desire to win means he will be an instant hit and I expect him to make an impact almost immediately”.

Ozil starts training from today and will be available to make his debut against Manchester United on the weekend.

Injury Updates

Goalkeeper Jack Butland has recovered from his broken finger and is available for selection this weekend. Having missed 2 weeks already Jack is looking forward to getting back between the sticks.

No injuries in defence so the boss has a full roster of players to chose from

Santi Cazorla is still unavailable while he recovers from ankle surgery and there is still no news on when he will return. Aaron Ramsey will miss the trip to old Trafford with a thigh injury but hopes to return for the midweek FA cup

Danny Welbeck will be out for 3 weeks after picking up a hamstring injury in the last game but all other strikers are cleared to play

New sponsorship deal imminent

The club have will shortly be announcing a major new sponsorship deal with local company Valley Tyres. The five year deal will start from next season and will include shirt sponsorship to coincide with the new kit. The deal is believed to be worth 5million per year with an additional £10 million bonus should the club progress to the champions league. Chief Executive Ivan Kronke, who played a key roll in signing the agreement, stated “This is a great opportunity to help promote a local company while also contributing to the success t the club. The additional fans will go towards transfers and improvements of the clubs facilities”. The club is also looking to use the funds to help bolster their online presence around the world and help promote the premier league worldwide.

Transfer Gossip

Alexis Sanchez has left the club to join Man City for a world record fee. Sanchez left in good spirits and is looking forward to spending his extra money

Everton midfielder Ross Barkley remains an key player of interest after he has failed to sign a new contract at the club.

Welsh youngster Ben Woodburn is currently being monitored by scouts and enquires are being made in regards to his availability

Lionel Messi has stated his desire to play in the premier league and has heaped praise on the way the Titans players and would love to play under the gaffer.

Vrigil Van Dyke has been offered a new 3 year contract with the club and the players agent is confident a new deal can be reached.
